Parallax Scrolling

Parallax scrolling gives the perception that the foreground is moving at a different rate than the backgorund. Click on the examples to take a look of some parrallax layouts.

The parallax effect

The icons are scrolling faster than the text

The parralax refers to the effect of the foreground scrolling faster than the background.

Web designers began incorporating parallax scrolling in 2011, using HTML5 and CSS3. Websites with parallax backgrounds are becoming[when?] an increasingly popular strategy, as advocates argue it is a simple way to embrace the fluidity of the Web.[16] Additionally, proponents use parallax backgrounds as a tool to better engage users and improve the overall experience that a website provides.[17] --wikipedia
